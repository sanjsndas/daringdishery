Our Story

From the embers of tradition to the flames of innovation, discover the philosophy and people behind The Daring Dishery.

The Genesis of a Culinary Rebellion

The Daring Dishery was born not in a brightly lit test kitchen, but in the liminal space between shadow and flame. Our founder, Morwenna Blackwood, spent years working in acclaimed restaurants across Europe, but always felt constrained by the conventions of fine dining. She yearned for something more primal, more honest, more connected to the elemental forces that transform raw ingredients into profound experiences.

In 2015, she abandoned her prestigious position at a Michelin-starred establishment and retreated to a converted forge in the Blackstone Valley. There, surrounded by the ghosts of industry and the raw materials of creation, she began experimenting with what would become the foundation of our culinary philosophy: the Raven Coal approach.

What began as a personal culinary journal shared with a handful of adventurous friends has grown into a community of thousands who share our belief that the most memorable meals are often those that challenge perception, that embrace a certain beautiful darkness, and that honor the transformative power of fire, patience, and daring.

Culinary Courage

We challenge culinary dogma and embrace techniques and combinations that others might consider too bold, too dark, or too unconventional. We believe that true innovation happens at the edges, not in the center.

The Raven Coal theme is not merely an aesthetic; it is our culinary doctrine. We revere the Raven for its intelligence, adaptability, and place in myth as a messenger between worlds. In our kitchen, this translates to recipes that are clever, layered, and transportive.

We honor Coal for its transformative potential. It is the raw, compressed energy of ancient forests, capable of producing intense, sustained heat. This is the foundation of our cooking techniques: the searing kiss of high heat, the slow, smoldering roast, the deep, penetrating warmth of a long braise. We are not afraid of char, of smoke, of the Maillard reaction pushed to its limit. This is where flavor is forged.

Meet Our Coven

The Daring Dishery is brought to life by a small but fiercely dedicated team of culinary artists, writers, and fire-tenders who share a passion for pushing boundaries and exploring the delicious darkness.

Morwenna Blackwood

Founder & Head Culinary Alchemist

With over 20 years in professional kitchens across Europe, Morwenna brings both technical mastery and rebellious spirit to The Daring Dishery. Her expertise in fire-based cooking techniques and forgotten preservation methods forms the backbone of our culinary philosophy.

Silas Crowe

Fire Master & Butchery Specialist

Silas honed his craft in the smokehouses of Tennessee and the asadors of Argentina before bringing his mastery of live-fire cooking to The Daring Dishery. He oversees all our fire-based techniques and develops our charcuterie and preservation recipes.

Lyra Nightshade

Forager & Botanical Curator

Lyra's knowledge of wild edibles, medicinal herbs, and unusual botanicals is unparalleled. She sources our most distinctive ingredients and develops our recipes for ferments, infusions, and botanical cocktails that capture the essence of the wild.

Our Commitment

At The Daring Dishery, we are committed to more than just creating extraordinary recipes. We strive to:

  • Source ingredients ethically and sustainably, with particular attention to heritage breeds, heirloom varieties, and wild-foraged components
  • Respect the whole animal and plant in our cooking, minimizing waste through preservation and creative use of overlooked parts
  • Share knowledge freely, empowering home cooks to experiment with techniques they might otherwise find intimidating
  • Build a community of adventurous eaters and cooks who appreciate depth, complexity, and culinary courage

Our mission is to guide you beyond the safe and the sunny. To show you that in the culinary shadows, there is unparalleled depth, unexpected beauty, and flavors that resonate with the primal core of what it means to cook and to feast.

So, stoke your fires, sharpen your knives, and join us. Be Daring.
